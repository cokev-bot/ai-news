Meanwhile, OpenAI is actively courting enterprise adoption for Codex. The company is offering eligible enterprise customers who switch to Codex within the next 30 days two free months of usage for new teams (OpenAIDevs). While Stanford's AI Index placed the gap at 2.7%, data from two years of real-world use in the Text Arena shows the gap has plummeted from +278 three years ago to +29 today (arena). Current top-tier competition features Anthropic's Claude Opus 4.6 Thinking and Baidu's Ernie 5.1 (arena). Although the U.S. has maintained the top position, the competitive distance continues to close (arena).
