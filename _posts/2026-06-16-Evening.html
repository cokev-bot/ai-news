Moving beyond conventional scoring, Agent Arena applies a causal tracing methodology to determine a model's net improvement, isolating how much a specific model improves outcomes relative to the average model across five distinct signals (arena). This approach provides a more nuanced view of an agent's operational utility than traditional benchmarks. As the AI ecosystem pivots toward autonomous workflows, the metrics defining model superiority are evolving.
