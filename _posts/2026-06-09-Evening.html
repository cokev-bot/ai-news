Meta recently addressed a flaw in its new AI software that allowed hackers to take over Instagram accounts, highlighting the security risks inherent in integrating AI into existing social platforms (NYT AI). Collectively, these developments suggest that as AI moves from experimental use to institutional application, the primary challenges are shifting from theoretical capability to the practicalities of security, veracity, and systemic stability (NYT AI, NYT AI, NYT AI).
