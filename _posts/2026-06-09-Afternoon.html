AI Labs

Summary: Anthropic has launched Claude Fable 5, a "Mythos-class" model described as the most capable model the company has ever made generally available (AnthropicAI). The release represents a significant shift in AI agent workflows; members of the Claude Code team report that the model's capabilities have transitioned their primary role from verifying that the AI performs tasks correctly to verifying that the AI is pursuing the correct objectives (ClaudeDevs). To manage the risks associated with this increased power, Claude Fable 5 includes new safety classifiers designed to flag prompts in "dual-use domains," specifically biotechnology and cybersecurity (ClaudeDevs). To prevent "dead-ending" user requests, the system now employs a fallback mechanism where refused requests are retried using Claude Opus 4.8 (ClaudeDevs). Users can access the model via the Claude Desktop app, the Claude Code CLI (version 2.1.170), or by using the `/model claude-fable-5` command (ClaudeDevs). Simultaneously, MiniMax is aggressively expanding its distribution and infrastructure footprint. The company is integrating with the RespanAI Gateway to provide easier developer access to its multimodal models (MiniMax_AI) and is collaborating with Modular to support the M3 model, which features a 1M-token context window and native multimodality (MiniMax_AI). MiniMax is also moving toward physical operations through a strategic partnership with SupplyAi and Maison Solutions to develop AI-native food supply chain solutions (MiniMax_AI, MiniMax_AI). Other notable ecosystem updates include OpenAI expanding its Responses API to support image results in web searches, enabling apps to surface visual references and products (OpenAIDevs), and the full rollout of Notebooks within the Gemini app across Europe (NotebookLM). Additionally, MiniMax is targeting the on-device AI market, with a scheduled appearance at the AiOS Meetup during WWDC '26 on June 11 (MiniMax_AI).

Summary: Anthropic has shifted the paradigm of AI development with the release of Claude Fable 5, its first generally available "Mythos-class" model (AnthropicAI, ClaudeDevs).
